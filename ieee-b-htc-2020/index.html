IEEE Bangalore Humanitarian Technology Conference (B-HTC) 2020 in association with IEEE Young Professionals Affinity Group and IEEE Bangalore Section brings to you a
”Panel Discussion” on Green Technology, Sustainable Development/Sustainable living and Green Education,
to be held during the conference scheduled from Oct 8-20, 2020
 
Theme: Millennials as the torch-bearer for Climate Change, with a focus on Technology, Education, and Sustainable Living.
 
We invite students and young professionals to submit their entries for being a part of this panel.
