STEP

Frequent Student transition & Elevation Partnership (STEP) programs have helped interested students learn about the young professionals and section activities and helped them transition from student membership to IEEE Young Professionals thus ensuring continuity of their membership. Students have also been apprised about India’s position in the field of research with the aim to equip them with the knowledge of technical writing and publishing under their #TechnicalWriteIndia campaign. We have also started training aspiring and budding engineers towards entrepreneurship in terms of bootcamps and workshops.

Mentor 4 You

Mentor 4 You is an initiative aimed at providing an opportunity for student members to interact with experts in their field of interest and through this initiative brought the students in touch with mentors who arer young professionals from the industry and academia who guide their mentees towards skill development and provide help and direction to their projects aimed at showing the path to mentees to acheive their goals

Student Leadership Program

During March Student Leadership Programme was Conducted in March at VVIET Mysore where around 150 students attended. Abhishek Appaji, Ashwini Appaji and Moinam Chatterjee delivered talks during this programme.

Vist To C-DAC 2020

IEEE Young Professionals Bangalore Section organized an Industry visit to C-DAC facility in Bengaluru during February 2020 for 50 students and young professionals from various student branches across Bangalore Section and from the industry.

Major Initiatives

Major partnerships with various developer communities:-

•IEEE societies such as Computer Society, Power and Energy society etc. and have initiated workshops such as Cyber Physical Workshop Series etc.

•Initiated their first annual young professional congress on 28th August 2015 at Christ University which was followed by their active involvement and organisation of the IEEE Region 10 Student/ Young-professionals/ Women in Engineering/ Life Member Congress from 25th-27th August, 2016 as part of IEEE Region10 Fifty Year Celebrations.

•Developed a strong connection with the industry and have aimed to strengthen the relevancy of IEEE Membership to industry.

•Organise numerous industrial visits to ISRO, Nokia, General Electric, CDAC etc. and various technical workshops spread across a variety of technical areas such as Machine Learning, Swift, Cloud Computing, Android etc. A part for ILS, WIECON conference.

Technical Writing and Publishing

The aim of this event was to provide impart knowledge of technical paper writing and publishing skills. The workshop also covered current scenario of research in India, factors to consider for publishing, where to publish, what is academic writing, ethics in technical writing, publishing, and many more. Since the workshop was hands-on, it helped them in knowing new platforms that they can work on.This event was conducted at NIE Mysore.

Team Building Activity

1. This trekking was held at Kunti Betta which is in Kachenahalli,Karnataka.

Technical Writing and Publishing

•This workshop was attended by 60 plus members (including faculties) which was held at SSIT, Tumkur. Students from different colleges of Tumkur Hub and Bangalore Hub were taken part.

• This workshop was conducted with the view, to encourage students in research field, and to help faculties in publishing their papers in various conferences so as pursue their PhD.

•The workshop had two sessions, First and Second. First session was all about publishing and Second was about writing technical paper. Whole session was handled by Abhishek Appaji.

Workshop

Ideation to Productization

IEEE Ideation to Productization was held on 29th July 2017 at B M S College of Engineering, Bengaluru. There were Six teams to pitch the ideas on their product development to the jury of 7 members. There were around 10 IEEE members and around 40 non-IEEE members. •Out of 6 pitches 1st prize was awarded to team Beckon with prize money of Rs.10000/- (team of 5) and 2nd prize was awarded to SARB with Rs.5,000/- (team of 3) as prize money rest of the 4 teams were given goodies to carry home. The event had Gala Lunch to all the participants and the guests who joined the event.

MIT Entrepreneurship Bootcamp at BMSCE
