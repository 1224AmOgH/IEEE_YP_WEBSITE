IEEE Bangalore Humanitarian Technology Conference (B-HTC) 2020

IEEE B-HTC 2020, Bangalore section in collaboration with BLDEA's V.P.Dr.P.G.Halakatti College of Engineering and Technology, Vijayapur, Karnataka and supported by IEEE India council is the first edition of this premier annual cross disciplinary conference that provides common platform to engineers, technologists, scientists, inventors, start-ups, academia and industry to discuss the recent advances in Humanitarian Technologies.

The Special Young Professionals (YPS) Track on Bridging Technology and Humanity consists of
1. Internet of Things
2. 5G and beyond
3. Artificial Intelligence
4. Machine Learning
5. Blockchain Technology
6. Nano Technology and
7. Intelligent Transport system
Papers with at least one author being Young Professionals (YP) can be submitted in this Track. Authors are requested to submit their research work in above themes (same paper should not be submitted in multiple tracks) using following enabling technologies (but not limited to):
• Satellite Communication, Navigation, Remote Sensing, Geoscience and Space Technology
• Antennas, RF & Microwave
• Robotics, Control systems and Automation
• Cloud computing, Bigdata, Architecture, Mobile and wireless communications, Optical and photonic communications, optical networks
• Biomedical engineering
• Circuits, systems and signal processing,
• Power electronics, machines and drives, high voltage
• Communication Technologies
• Semiconductor, Photonics and Nanotechnology
• IoT and enabling technologies
• Information and communication technologies
